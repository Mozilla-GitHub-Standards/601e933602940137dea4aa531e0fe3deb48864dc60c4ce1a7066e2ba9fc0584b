Welcome to he Mozilla Teaching & Learning Community

We are informal educators, teachers, techies, learners, innovators and explorers invested in having impact on the open web through by contributing our skills to the Mozilla project including:

  • Participation
  • MDN
  • Mozilla Foundation
  • FSA
  • Tech Speakers

Find us!

  • On Rocket Chat - say hello! (coming soon)
  • On Github - grab a task!

We volunteer skills in areas of:

  • Instructional Design
  • Facilitation
  • User Research
  • Accessibility Testing for Learning Content
  • Technical Writing
  • Proof Reading/QA
  • Online Training
  • Workshop Development
  • Content Curation

We Collaborate With:

  • Projects & community, individuals and teams who need their learning content tested in the real world.
  • Projects & community, individuals and teams who need to explore innovation in content development & delivery including user research.
  • Projects & community, individuals and teams with small tasks, and requests for help that hep them reach their goals for impact on Mozilla's mission.
  • Community & staff in need of mentoring and coaching in development-of and delivery-of learning content for specific audiences, and outcomes.
  • Community groups like the Design Community - to investigate ways we can work together on bigger projects.
